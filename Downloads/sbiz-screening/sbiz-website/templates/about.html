About Sbiz

Sbiz started as a Colab notebook and grew into a small but real piece of business software: a public-facing site, and an HR assistant that reads resumes so your team doesn't have to read every line of every one.

It's built with Flask, deployed on Render, and powered by Claude for the parts that need to actually understand a document.
